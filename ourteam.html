How Can You Contribute

Volunteer

Feel free to reach out to us with your tech blogs or details about any workshops or webinars you want to host related to your personal growth or your preparation journey for various tech scholarships and conferences. So that our aspiring students can hear your empowering voice and they can learn from your wisdom. You can also volunteer to handle our social media handles and maintain the database.

Resource Team

You can get involved in our team of change-makers and impact the female tech community, a network strong enough to make a positive change in women's lives. You will be expected to search for Scholarships, Fellowships, and other related content and maintain a database to help keep the community updated.

Web Developer

If you want to work on enhancing our website and experience the ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment, this is the right place for you. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced developer, you are welcome to become a part of this community and contribute to the growth of women in tech.
